    Standard Curriculum

    Kinder (3-6) Years

    The Kinder Program is designed to provide a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment for children aged 3-6 years. The program focuses on the development of the whole child, with an emphasis on social, emotional, cognitive and physical development.

    Elementary School

    The Elementary School Program is designed to provide a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment for children aged 6-12 years. The program focuses on the development of the whole child, with an emphasis on social, emotional, cognitive and physical development.

    Middle (10-16) Years

    The Middle School Program is designed to provide a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment for children aged 10-16 years. The program focuses on the development of the whole child, with an emphasis on social, emotional, cognitive and physical development.
